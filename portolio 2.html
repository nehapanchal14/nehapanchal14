About me

I work at the intersection of People Programs, Learning & Development, and employee experience — with a focus on building environments where people can grow, contribute meaningfully, and feel connected to their work.

I bring structure, strategy, and heart to development initiatives, from onboarding and learning programs to employee engagement and leadership experiences. What I love most is turning big ideas into practical, well-run programs that support both people and business outcomes.

One of my core beliefs is that growth and change are rarely comfortable — but they're often the catalyst for something new. That belief shapes how I design programs: with honesty about the process and genuine care for the people going through it.

I'm especially drawn to organizations where HR and L&D are seen as strategic partners — where learning, culture, and positive impact truly go hand in hand. I'm currently at Abbott Diabetes Care and open to the right next opportunity in Talent Development or People Programs.
